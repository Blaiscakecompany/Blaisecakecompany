pistachio cherry cake

ingredient

  • MILK: 128 GMS
  • BUTTER: 142 GMS
  • FLOUR: 199 GMS
  • PISTACHEO PASTE: 71 GMS
  • EGG YOLK: 421 GMS
  • WHOLE EGGS: 142 GMS
  • EGG WHITES: 355 GMS
  • SUGAR: 170 GMS

baking instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Combine milk and pistachio paste.
  3. Cream butter and sugar.
  4. Add egg yolks.
  5. Mix in flour.
  6. Blend in whole eggs.
  7. Fold mixtures 23456 together.
  8. Beat egg whites; fold in.
  9. Pour batter into pans.
  10. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
